When meeting someone for the first time in person, please remember to:

  • Insist on a public meeting place like a cafe.
  • Do not meet in a secluded place, or invite strangers into your home.
  • Be especially careful when donating high value items.
  • Tell a friend or family member where you're going.
  • Take your cell phone along if you have one.
  • Consider having a friend accompany you.
  • Trust your instincts.
  • Be extra careful if they ask for money.
  • If you want to give them money then only give the person cash so they don't take your identity through checks or credit cards.

Taking these simple precautions helps make things safer for everyone.
